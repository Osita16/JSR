Process of Creating a 3D Model

Below are the detailed steps to generate an accurate 3D model of the building using AGI Metashape software:

  1. Extract Frames from Video: The first step involves converting the video into multiple high-quality frames for detailed analysis.
  2. Align Frames: Use AGI Metashape to align the frames, ensuring that all captured angles seamlessly fit together.
  3. Generate Dense Cloud: Create a dense cloud point to accurately capture the building's geometry and structure.
  4. Build Cloud Mesh: Develop a mesh structure from the dense cloud, forming the building's basic 3D representation.
  5. Texture Refinement: Refine the texture of the 3D model to enhance its visual appeal and make it as realistic as possible.

Advantages of 3D Modeling from Drone Footage

The use of drone footage and 3D modeling offers several benefits:

  • Comprehensive Analysis: A 360° perspective provides detailed insights into every aspect of the building.
  • Time Efficiency: The automated process significantly reduces the time required for manual measurements and modeling.
  • Accuracy: Advanced software like AGI Metashape ensures precise alignment and realistic texture mapping.
  • Applications: The model can be used in urban planning, construction, forensic investigations, and more.
